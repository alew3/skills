# Style Board Template

Defines the project look (palette + lighting + grade + art style) as continuity constraints reused by every later generation.

PROJECT: [name]
ART STYLE: [photoreal | painterly concept | cel-shaded anime | flat vector | …] (not necessarily "cinematic")
ASPECT RATIO: [16:9 default]

LOOK BIBLE:
- Palette: [5–6 colors / hex]
- Lighting: [key direction + quality, mood]
- Grade / film look: [e.g. teal-orange, desaturated, Kodak Portra, bleach bypass]
- Texture / material cues: [surfaces, grain]

SEND VERBATIM (single board, labeled tiles):
[Prompt for a grid sheet: 5–6 palette swatch tiles, 2 material/texture tiles, 2 lighting/atmosphere studies, 2–3 representative mini-vignettes in the chosen style; cohesive palette + lighting across all tiles; each tile labeled; flat presentation, neutral background.]

NEGATIVE: no inconsistent palette across tiles, no unrelated styles.
